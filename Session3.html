Game Overview

Persona is a popular role-playing video game series developed by Atlus. It features a mix of dungeon crawling, social simulation, and turn-based combat elements. The game is known for its deep storytelling and unique art style.

Characters

Protagonist

The main character, often a high school student, who must balance daily life and battling supernatural forces.

Supporting Characters

Various friends and allies who join the protagonist on their journey, each with their own unique abilities and storylines.

Storyline

The Persona series explores themes of identity, self-discovery, and the power of human connections. Players engage in a gripping narrative where they confront their inner selves and unravel mysteries surrounding supernatural phenomena.
